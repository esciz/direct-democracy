A live stream of the meetings for City Council, Redevelopment Agency and Planning Commission is available while they are in session. Video archives of previous meetings are also available.

To access agendas, backup information, or minutes, please refer to the date of the meeting:

  • All City Council, Redevelopment Agency, Special Meetings, Planning Commission, and Board and Commission agendas from November 10, 2011, through present
  • For meetings not available on the above link please visit our Records Page or Contact the City Clerk's Office at 702-267-1410.
Public Comment

RDA, City Council Committee, City Council Regular

Public Comment or comments relating to Redevelopment Agency (RDA), City Council Committee, or City Council Regular agenda items may be submitted by mail or Contact Henderson before 5:30 p.m. the day before the scheduled meeting. 

There will be two designated public comment periods. The first will be that the beginning of the meeting, prior to any action items, and will be designated for comment relating to items appearing on the agenda. The second will be at the end of the meeting, prior to adjournment, and is for general public comment. All public comment guidelines outlined on the agenda will apply.

Planning Commission

Public Comment or comments relating to Planning Commission may be submitted the day before the scheduled meeting using the links below or through Contact Henderson. Comments can also be submitted via email to (Planning Commission) cdcoordinators@cityofhenderson.com prior to the meeting.  Be sure to include your name, zip code, the agenda item number on which you are providing your comment, along with your comment.

Request an Interpreter or Communication Services for Public Meetings or Programs

The City of Henderson is committed to compliance with the Americans with Disabilities Act and Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act and is dedicated to providing equal access to all city-sponsored programs, services and activities for citizens with disabilities.

City Council and Redevelopment Agency meetings are held in the Council Chambers. If you need an accommodation to attend or participate in this meeting, arrangements for a sign language interpreter or services necessary for effective communication for qualified persons with disabilities should be made as soon as possible, but no later than 72 hours before the scheduled meeting. Listening devices are available for persons with hearing impairments.

For City Council and Redevelopment Agency Meetings, please contact the City Clerk's office at 702-267-1400 or TTY: 7-1-1, at least 72 hours in advance to request a sign language interpreter. You may also submit your request using the Contact Henderson icon.

For Planning Commission meetings, please contact Community Development and Services Department at 702-267-1500 or TTY: 7-1-1, at least 72 hours in advance to request a sign language interpreter. You may also submit your request using the Contact Henderson icon.
