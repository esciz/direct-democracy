Upcoming Meetings

Board and Committee Meetings

All meetings of the Board of Regents and its committees are open to the public and, according to the Nevada Open Meeting Law, must have posted agendas at least three business days prior to the meeting. Meeting on the campuses enables the Regents to learn more about the institutions they govern as well as have greater access to students, faculty and staff.
