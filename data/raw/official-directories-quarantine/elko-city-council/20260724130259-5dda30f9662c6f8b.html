City Council

Elko isn't just a place to live; it's a place to thrive. Our commitment to fostering a sense of community, recreation, and sustainable growth makes Elko an exceptional place to call home. Whether you're here to work, play, or simply explore, we invite you to experience all that Elko has to offer. Welcome to our city – where opportunities are boundless, and the community is at the heart of it all.
